Designs & Fabrication

Mechanical fixture design, robotic cell integration, and iterative assembly-driven improvements.

Mechanical Design & CAD

  • Design of mechanical structures, robotic cells, and automation systems.
  • Creation of 3D models, assemblies, and technical drawings.
  • Sheet metal design including flat patterns, bending considerations, and tolerancing.
  • Design of custom end-effectors, grippers, fixtures, and support structures.
  • Creation of BOMs and structured documentation for production.

Assembly & System Integration

  • Mechanical assembly of multi-component industrial systems.
  • Electrical cabinet integration and wiring coordination.
  • Robot installation, anchoring, and commissioning.
  • Integration of robots with PLCs, HMIs, sensors, actuators, and CNC machines.
  • Debugging mechanical misalignments and real-world deviations from CAD.
  • Iterative design improvements based on assembly feedback.

Robotics & Automation

  • Industrial robot programming, including motion types, routines, and cycle optimization.
  • Development of automatic and manual operation modes.
  • Sensor integration and signal mapping.
  • Control philosophy definition and industrial communications integration.
  • End-effector design and performance validation.
  • Conveyor system design, tensioning, drive alignment, and load validation.

Testing & Validation

  • Factory Acceptance Testing (FAT).
  • Workspace analysis and reachability validation.
  • Performance testing under load and endurance conditions.
  • Safety validation, mode-switching logic testing, and edge-case handling.
  • System recalibration and parameter tuning.
  • Documentation of improvement points for future revisions.

About

I am a robotics-focused engineer with experience in system integration, mechanical design, and ROS2-based development in C++/Python, contributing to industrial automation projects from design to deployment.

I have explored biomechanics and musculoskeletal modeling through academic and independent projects, with a growing interest in exoskeletons and prosthetics.

My focus is on developing robotic systems for human-centered assistive technologies, particularly wearable robotics.
